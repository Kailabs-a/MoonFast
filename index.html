True Hunger SOS

Physical hunger vs. Cravings

If you are truly physically hungry and cannot sleep without eating, choose a small snack that combines protein and fiber to stabilize blood sugar:

  • Greek Yogurt High in protein and soothing for the stomach.
  • Apple Slices + Peanut Butter Provides fiber and healthy fats.
  • Handful of Almonds Quietly satisfies the "crunch" craving.
  • Air-Popped Popcorn High volume and low calorie (skip the butter).
